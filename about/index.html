About

Fisayo Fosudo, also known professionally as Fisayo, is a Nigerian YouTuber, best known for his technology-focused videos.

The channel, whose name is a combination of his first and last name as "Fisayo Fosudo" has over 26 thousand subscribers and over 1 million total video views.

In 2018, Fisayo was nominated for two awards in Nigeria, namely "The Future Awards Africa Nigeria Prize for New Media" by The Future Awards Africa,[1] and "City People Music Award for Vlogger of the Year" by City People Music Awards.[2]
